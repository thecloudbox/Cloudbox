## Usage Guidelines

### Colors:
- Terminal Blue: #3b82f6
- Sea Green (Hover): #10b981
- Slate Background: #0f172a
- Text White: #f1f5f9

### Spacing:
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to 1/4 of the logo height
- Do not distort or modify the aspect ratio

### Background Compatibility:
All PNG files have transparent backgrounds and work on any background color. For best results:
- Use on dark backgrounds (#000000 to #1e293b)
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background
